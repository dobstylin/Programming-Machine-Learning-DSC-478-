Pep Vs No Pep¶

Age¶

The average customer who buys a Pep is about 5 years older than the average customer who does not buy the Pep product. The mean of customers who buy the Pep is 45.13 years of age, while the mean age of customers who do not buy the Pep is 40.1 years of age. For customers who purchase the Pep product, the 3rd quartile is 58 years of age and the 1st quartile is 34 years of age, which gives IQR of 24 years. For customers who do not purchase the Pep product, the 3rd quartile is 51 years of age, and the 1st quartile is 27 years of age, which gives an IQR of 24 years also. This shows that customers who buy the Pep product tend to be older than customers who do not buy the Pep product.

Income¶

The average income for customers who purchase a Pep product is 5,743.97 more than the income of customers who have not purchased a Pep product. The average income for customers who purchased a Pep product is 30,644.92 and the average income for customers who did not purchase a Pep product is 24,900.95. The 1st and 3rd quartile for customers who purchased a Pep product are 19,639.48 and 38,458.35 respectively, with an IQR of 18,818.87. The 1st and 3rd quartile for customers who did not purchase a Pep product are 15,366.48 and 31,210.90 respectively, with an IQR of 15,844.42. This shows that customers who purchase the Pep product tend to earn a higher income than customers who do not purchase a Pep product.

Children¶

The mean number of children for customers who do not use the Pep product (1.07) is slightly higher than the mean number of children for customers who use the Pep product(0.94). Additionally, customers who purchase a Pep product have a 3rd quartile number of students of 1, while customers who did not purchase the Pep product have a 3rd quartile of 2 children. This shows that less than 25% of customers who use the Pep product have more than 1 child. This indicates that customers or potential customers who have 1 child or less are more likely to be interested in the Pep product than customers or potential customers with more than 1 child.

Gender¶

There are 144 male customers and 130 female customers who purchase the Pep product. There are 170 male customers and 156 female customers who do not use the Pep product. About 46% of current male customers use the Pep proudct and about 45% of current female customers use the Pep product. There are more male customers overall, and more male than female customers that both use and do not use the Pep product.

Region¶

123 inner city customers out of 269 total inner city customers use the Pep product, this makes up about 46% of inner city customers. There are 71 out of a total of 173 customers from towns that use the Pep product, this makes up about 41% of customers from towns. There are 46 out of 96 customers from rural areas that use the Pep product, which makes up almost 48% of rural customers. There are 34 out of 62 customers from suburban regions that use the Pep product, this makes up almost 56% of suburban customers. This shows that of the current customers, suburban customers are the most likely to use the Pep product, with customers from towns being the least likely to use the Pep product.

Married¶

Of the 396 married customers, 154 use the Pep product, this give us the percentage of about 39%. Of the 204 unmarried customers, 120 use the Pep product, or 59%. This shows that of the current customers, unmarried customers are more likely to purchase the Pep product.

Car¶
